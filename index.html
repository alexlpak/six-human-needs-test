6 Human Needs Test by Cloe Madanes

Welcome to the unofficial online version of the 6 Human Needs Test!
This test will help you find your top Human Needs out of the following categories:

  • • Certainy
  • • Uncertainty
  • • Significance
  • • Love & Connection
  • • Growth
  • • Contribution
How To Take The Test Honestly consider each statement and check the option for:
  • Yes for "yes, this is really me"
  • Partly for "this is partly how I am"
  • No for "this probably not how I am"
  • (If you don't know whether it applies it's usually best to check "no")
Please note: Your progress will be saved automatically as you go.
